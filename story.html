Our Company

The Sweetgrass Buffalo Company, based in beautiful Melville, Montana, was established in 1997. We've seen a lot over the years, but we've stuck around because of a simple principle - we put pride into our products.

Our Meats

Wwe've specialized in only the finest 100% Buffalo meat products. Our Buffalo graze naturally on open range grasslands in a ranch setting. Our Buffalo is hand selected and cut to ensure the finest flavor. We carefully package our product using a 3 Step Packaging Process that guarantees freshness from our door to your table.
