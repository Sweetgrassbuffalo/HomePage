Grilling Recommendations

Cooking time is important in order not to overcook your steaks. Total cooking time will depend on the thickness of the steaks and the temperature of the grill or pan.

Steak Rare Medium
1" thick 6-8 min 10-12 min
1½" thick 10-12 min 14-18 min
2" thick 14-20 min 20-25 min

Well done Bison steaks are not recommended. Due to the leanness of the meat, Bison has a tendency to become dry when overcooked.

Grass fed Buffalo Meat is the Best you can get

Here in Sweetgrass County Montana, with the meadows and hills full of native prairie grass we have most natural and best feed possible for our buffalo on the Tanaka Land and Livestock ranch. We live here with the animals, and tend to their needs daily. While one does not handle buffalo all of the time, it is important to be with the herd to see how things are going and correct problems early.

Our buffalo are raised in the same natural surroundings they have been found in for historically. As a result, it is some of the finest meat available today. Lean, juicy and flavorful. There is no such thing as a bad cut of buffalo meat. We guarantee you will be satisfied with your purchase.
