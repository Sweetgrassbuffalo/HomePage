Grilling Recommendations

Cooking time is important in order not to overcook your steaks. Total cooking time will depend on the thickness of the steaks and the temperature of the grill or pan.

Steak Rare Medium
1" thick 6-8 min 10-12 min
1½" thick 10-12 min 14-18 min
2" thick 14-20 min 20-25 min

Well done Buffalo steaks are not recommended. Due to the leanness of the meat, Buffalo has a tendency to become dry when overcooked.

Grass Fed Buffalo Meat is Better.

While Buffalo meat tastes similar - though slightly sweeter - to a high quality cut of beef, Buffalo meat stands out as being much higher in nutrition. Buffalo contributes about 69% more iron to your diet than beef; with higher levels of vitamins, minerals, and twice as much Beta-Carotene as grain-fed meats. It's also high in essential fatty acids and has an excellent ratio of Omega-3 to Omega-6 acids. Grass fed Buffalo are allowed to roam free and fed at will throughout the day on the abundant grass filled meadows of the Tatanka Land and Livestock company's ranch.

All of our buffalo cuts are hand selected and cut in Sweetgrass County, Montana. Our packaging process ensures freshness from our door to your table. We take great pride in the quality of our cuts and feel that our meats are truly the best money can buy.
